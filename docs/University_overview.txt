About University:
Aditya University is a multidisciplinary private university in Andhra Pradesh offering undergraduate, postgraduate, diploma, executive, and doctoral programs.

The university evolved from the Aditya educational ecosystem and focuses on industry-oriented education, innovation, placements, research, and practical learning.
