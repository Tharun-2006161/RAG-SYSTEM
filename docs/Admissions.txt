ADITYA UNIVERSITY ADMISSIONS KNOWLEDGE BASE

GENERAL ADMISSION PROCESS

Step 1:
Visit official portal:
https://apply.adityauniversity.in

Step 2:
Create account with:
- full name
- mobile number
- email
- password

Step 3:
Select desired program

Step 4:
Fill admission form

Step 5:
Upload documents

Step 6:
Submit application

Step 7:
Fee payment

Step 8:
Verification + confirmation

ADMISSION MODES

Admissions may happen through:
1. entrance exam route
2. merit route
3. direct application route (if officially applicable)

UG ADMISSION ELIGIBILITY

For Engineering:
Candidate must complete 10+2 with:
- Physics
- Mathematics
- Chemistry

Accepted entrance exams:
- AP EAPCET
- JEE Main
- university admission pathways

PG ADMISSIONS

MBA:
Eligibility:
Any recognized bachelor degree

Accepted exams:
- AP ICET
- equivalent eligibility route

MCA:
Eligibility:
Graduation with mathematics background

Accepted exams:
- AP ICET

M.Tech:
Eligibility:
Relevant engineering degree

Accepted exams:
- GATE
- AP PGECET

PhD:
Eligibility:
Relevant postgraduate qualification

DOCUMENTS REQUIRED

- 10th memo
